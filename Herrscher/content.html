Herrscher is a term used for beings considered to be apostles of Honkai with most of them taking human forms, inhabiting human vessels or have non-human appearance. Their powers are generated from their Herrscher Core which draws its power from the Imaginary Space. They effortlessly transcend human capabilities, are capable of bending worldly laws to their whim and command entire armies of zombies and Honkai Beasts, to overwhelm and destroy human civilization.

Pseudo-Herrschers

Pseudo-Herrschers refer to false Herrschers. Herrschers that are not part of the Herrscher cycle, and were either created artificially or through possession. Normally Pseudo-Herrschers are made with residual Honkai energy, and are thus less powerful than an average Herrscher.
